Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work ?

Come Over allows you to share a video file as a stream to other people, and watch it together. On the homepage, you can create a new stream, and share the link to anyone. The creator of the stream then has full control, and can pause/seek a particular time in the video file: it will get synchronized with all the viewers !

Yes, but How does it work ?

Come Over uses the WebTorrent library, which can transform any browser into a peer2peer client and server. Combined with live streaming, it is now possible to share streams to other people, without any third-party server.

Is it legal ?

Come Over only provides tools for people to share video streams with each other. Come over is not hosting, nor transmitting any data, hence the users endorse the entire responsibility of the content they share.

Are there any limitations ?

Yes ! Since this is peer2peer streaming, sharing the content entirely depends on upload and download capacity of each peer, as well as the geographical distance between peers. Also, since browsers natively limit connections between peers, there might be a maximum amount of users that can access the room. There is no precise data on this, but it should be between 256 and 500 peers, on Google Chrome.
